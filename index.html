Who I Am

I'm Raghav — an AI researcher, technologist, and cross-cultural problem-solver based in Dublin, Ireland. My journey has taken me from Computer Science at TU Dublin to an Erasmus exchange in Finland, and now an internship at Utrecht University building AI for legal aid.

I'm fascinated by how technology shapes human behaviour and where we will take AI next. My work focuses on building AI systems that people can actually trust and use — whether that's helping migrants navigate legal systems or supporting patients with chronic kidney disease.

Outside work, you'll find me hiking Irish trails, ice skating, performing live vocals, or getting deliberately lost in a new city just to find my way back.
